01 / About

Who I Am

I'm a bilingual finance and operations systems architect. I design and implement the controls, data flows, and reporting systems that make organizations run clean, fast, and verifiable.

My foundation is military leadership. As a U.S. Army NCO and team leader, I ran daily operations and reporting and maintained accountability for equipment valued above $2M. In the Department of Defense, I managed multi-site budgets above $5M annually, supervised 50+ military and civilian staff, and built internal controls that produced zero material findings in federal reviews.

I've also operated in the private sector, where speed, customer expectations, and profitability are non-negotiable. That mix taught me how to build systems that satisfy compliance without slowing the mission.

Now I'm deepening the technical layer through my Master's in Information Systems, and I build the tooling using Python, SQL, and lightweight web apps to automate recurring reporting, reduce manual errors, and turn raw data into dashboards leaders actually use.

If an organization wants fewer surprises, tighter controls, and faster decision-making, I don't just manage the work. I build the system that makes the work reliable.
